Slow upstream service

Connections timed out before finishing

This usually happens when a third-party utility API is under load.

What this means

We reached out to a rate or weather API, but it didn’t reply within the allowed window.

Quick next steps

  • Retry after a few seconds—results often succeed on the second attempt.
  • Check your connection or switch networks if you’re on weak Wi-Fi.
  • Contact Support if timeouts persist for a specific ZIP or account.
